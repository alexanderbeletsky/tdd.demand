EPAM Systems (www.epam.com), one of the largest Software Engineering Services Companies in European IT market, CMMI Level 4 certified, is looking for Senior .NET Developer for its Kyiv Office.


Project

The project is a new Software-as-a-Service enterprise platform developed and designed to operate at cloud environment.

Customer is one of the leading providers on American and world markets in mining industry.

Solution is a large-scale maintenance management system aimed to help better maintain the health of resources and equipment (including real-time notifications, alarms, trend analysis for long-term monitoring etc.)


Requirements:

  • 4+ years of commercial software engineering experience (C#, .NET platform)
  • Azure
  • MS SQL Server / SQL Azure (including SQL Server Reporting and Analysis Services)
  • WCF/WCF Data Services / WCF REST
  • Entity Framework
  • Strong understanding of OOP/ODD and design patterns principles (including dependency injection, IoC and other enterprise design patterns)
  • Good understanding and experience with unit testing, TDD
  • Good oral and written level of English (candidate should be able to report progress and raise issues/questions on calls)

As a plus:

  • .NET Compact Framework experience
  • Java (J2EE) experience

Company offers:

  • Competitive compensation depending on experience and skills
  • Financial stability
  • Individual career path
  • Paid vacation according to the Ukrainian laws, 100% paid sick list
  • Social package - 100% compensation (medical insurance, sports, etc.)
  • Technology and business trainings
  • Experienced specialists are provided with an opportunity to be promoted to Project Managers & Team Leaders
  • Opportunities for self-improvement: electronic libraries, seminars, conferences
  • 50% compensation of the cost of certification
  • 50% compensation of the cost of IT conferences
  • English classes with native speaker (certified English teacher)
  • Convenient office location in the center
  • Friendly team, loyalty and understanding
  • Flexible working schedule
